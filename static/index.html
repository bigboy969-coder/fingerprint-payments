How it works
Three steps.

The simplest checkout experience ever built. No cards, no phones, no PINs.

Enroll once

Customer scans the QR code at the terminal, enters their card details privately on their own phone, then scans their finger. Takes 30 seconds.

2

Every visit after that — just place a finger on the scanner. FingerPay identifies who they are in under a second.

Stripe charges their card on file automatically. Money goes straight to your account. No card swipe, no phone tap, no PIN.

Instant checkout

Payments complete in under 3 seconds. Faster than tapping a card or unlocking a phone. Reduces queue time significantly.

Bank-level security

Fingerprints are encrypted with AES-256-GCM and stored in a separate database. Raw fingerprint data never leaves your terminal.

FingerPay is an authentication layer on top of Stripe. Your customers' cards are stored securely by Stripe — the world's most trusted payments infrastructure.
